About Me

Nguyen Duc Anh

Front-End Developer

I am an Information Technology graduate specializing in Construction Informatics, with hands-on experience in web development, API design, and database management. I have developed interfaces and APIs for e-commerce websites using tools like React, Thymeleaf, and the .NET Framework, always prioritizing functionality, consistency, and reliability. Additionally, I am proficient in programming languages such as C# and VB.Net, and I hold certifications in English (IELTS 5.5). Driven to continuously improve, I am committed to enhancing my technical and language skills (English and Japanese). In my free time, I enjoy learning 3D animation with CSS, exploring new programming techniques, and listening to music.
